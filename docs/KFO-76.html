76 New Year's Eve at Overton

There would be a merry company gathered in the home, awaiting the coming of the patron Saint Belschnickel!

He Would finally come, + throw nuts out for all

When little, mother was afraid of him. Ide was so big + gruff. She would stick close to Anna Leighty .
